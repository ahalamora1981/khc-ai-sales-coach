INNOVATION HIGHLIGHT 07

Predictive Coaching & Pattern Mining

PREDICTIVE COACHING Proactive Preparation

AI identifies knowledge gaps before sales visits. Pushes relevant content based on upcoming customer context. Transforms reactive learning into proactive preparation.

"You're visiting a Sichuan hotpot chain tomorrow. Based on your memory, here's a 5-minute briefing on chili-based products and three successful case studies."

PATTERN MINING Success Replication

Aggregates successful patterns across the sales team. Identifies what works and replicates it. Creates a living knowledge base of proven strategies.

"Manager Zhang exceeded targets 3 months consecutively in hotpot: lead with cost-per-serving analysis, then demonstrate taste differentiation. Close rate: 85%."

CONTEXTUAL LEARNING In the Flow of Work

Learning triggered by real situations — not scheduled modules. Knowledge is immediately applicable. Content surfaces exactly when the rep needs it.

"Customer mentions switching to a competitor? AI instantly provides competitive talking points, switching-cost analysis, and relevant success stories."

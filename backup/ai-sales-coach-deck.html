Kraft Heinz China seeks to develop an AI-Powered Sales Coach Chatbot to empower its field sales team in the foodservice sauce category — an intelligent, always-available assistant that helps representatives learn product knowledge, receive personalized coaching, and make data-driven product recommendations based on restaurant menu analysis.

The Challenge 02 / 16

A Strategic
Gap

KHC is a global leader in ketchup, but aims to become a leader in the Chinese sauce market. The field sales team needs better tools to compete against local giants like Haitian and Lee Kum Kee.

China's condiment market: ¥4,981 billion (2024), growing at 7.0% CAGR. Compound seasonings — KHC's growth area — are growing at 10.2% CAGR.

Core Innovation 03 / 16
The Differentiator

Personal Memory
Architecture

Not a generic AI chatbot — YOUR AI coach. Each user has a dedicated memory that records their experience, preferences, and learning outcomes, dynamically retrieved and updated with every interaction.

01 · Extraction Learn from
Every Interaction
  • What products did the user ask about?
  • What recommendations were accepted?
  • What learning modules were completed?
  • What customer feedback was received?

Product Preferences Learning Progress Success Patterns Customer Feedback Weakness Areas Context History
03 · Evolve Continuous
Evolution
  • Old patterns updated with new data
  • Confidence scores adjust based on outcomes
  • Irrelevant memories decay over time
  • New insights incorporated continuously

Innovation 04 / 16
Intelligence

Intelligent Menu
Analysis

Photo recognition + OCR + taste profile analysis — not just "what products to use" but "why they fit this menu", with innovation suggestions for new menu items.

Photo → OCR → Dish Recognition → Taste Analysis → KHC Match
Real-World Example

"I've analysed this Sichuan-style restaurant menu. Taste Profile: Spicy (7/10), Numbing (8/10), Savory (6/10). Recommendation: KHC Chili Garlic Sauce — perfect for enhancing Mapo Tofu's heat profile while adding depth."

Innovation Suggestion Fusion Mapo Tofu

Suggest a 'Fusion Mapo Tofu' using our Chili Garlic Sauce + a touch of Italian herbs. This differentiation could attract younger diners while maintaining authenticity. Similar restaurants saw a 20% increase in average ticket size.

Two Modes Capture or Type

Primary: Photo-based menu recognition using the phone camera.
Secondary: Manual text input as a reliable fallback.

Innovation
PC
Predictive Intelligence

Predictive
Coaching

AI proactively identifies knowledge gaps before sales visits
and pushes relevant content based on upcoming customer context.

Example

"You're visiting a Sichuan hotpot chain tomorrow. Based on your memory, you haven't worked with spicy sauce applications much. Here's a 5-minute briefing on our chili-based products and three successful case studies from similar restaurants."

How It Works
  • Calendar/visit schedule analysed
  • Memory checked for knowledge gaps
  • Relevant content identified from KB
  • Personalised coaching brief generated
  • Push notification sent before visit

Innovations 05 / 16

More Innovation
Highlights

Innovation 04 Success Pattern
Mining

Aggregates successful patterns across the sales team. Identifies what works and replicates it. Creates a living knowledge base of proven strategies.

"Manager Zhang has exceeded targets for 3 consecutive months in the hotpot segment. Here's his playbook."

Innovation 05 Contextual
Learning

Learning happens in the flow of work, not in a classroom. Content is triggered by real situations, not scheduled modules. Knowledge is immediately applicable.

Real-time competitive talking points, switching cost analysis, and success stories — right when you need them.

Innovation 06 Continuous Evolution

The system gets smarter with every interaction. Recommendations improve based on acceptance/rejection data. Knowledge gaps are automatically identified and addressed.

Key Differentiator

Each innovation builds on the Personal Memory foundation — the system learns your strengths, weaknesses, preferences, and successful patterns, providing increasingly personalised guidance over time.

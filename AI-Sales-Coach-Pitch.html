PROPOSED SOLUTION 06

Core Innovation: Personal Memory Architecture

The KHC Sales Coach is a mobile-first, AI-powered assistant that transforms how field sales representatives learn, prepare, and execute customer interactions. Unlike traditional training platforms, our solution provides contextual, personalized, and evolving guidance.

STEP 01 Memory Extraction Every interaction is analyzed to extract product interests, recommendations accepted, and learning completed.
STEP 02 Personal Memory Store Six memory dimensions: Product Preferences, Learning Progress, Success Patterns, Customer Feedback, Weakness Areas, Context History.
STEP 03 Continuous Evolution Patterns update with new data, confidence scores adjust based on outcomes, and irrelevant memories decay over time.

RISK MITIGATION 17

Identified Risks & Mitigations

KNOWLEDGE BASE QUALITY

Risk: Insufficient content quality in knowledge sources

Mitigation: Early assessment of content; recommend content remediation if needed

AI HALLUCINATION

Risk: Incorrect product recommendations or information

Mitigation: Human-in-the-loop validation; confidence scoring; grounding in knowledge base

USER ADOPTION

Risk: Low adoption among field sales team

Mitigation: Involve sales reps in design; gamification; manager sponsorship

DATA PRIVACY

Risk: PIPL compliance and data security concerns

Mitigation: PIPL compliance audit; data encryption; access controls; mainland China deployment

IMAGE RECOGNITION ACCURACY

Risk: OCR and dish recognition may have accuracy issues with Chinese menus

Mitigation: Use mature OCR + LLM pipeline; fallback to text input; continuous model improvement

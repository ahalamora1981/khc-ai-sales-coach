## Problem Statement

Foodservice Sales:

- I want to have an AI chatbot to teach me how to sell KHC sauce to restaurant.
- AI chat robot has self-learning and recommend menu to me.
- I input the menu of this restaurant, and AI chat robot can recommend KHC sauce in the taste application and innovation recommendation for consumer taste
